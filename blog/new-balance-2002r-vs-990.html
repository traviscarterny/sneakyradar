NEW BALANCE 2002R VS 990

Updated Mar 2026 · Prices from StockX, GOAT, eBay & Amazon
2002R vs 990 Showdown

The New Balance 2002R and 990 are two of the most popular sneakers in the game right now — but they serve different purposes at different price points. If you're trying to decide between them, here's the complete breakdown.

Compare prices: Search 2002R or Search 990 on SneakyRadar to find the best deal across StockX, GOAT, eBay, and Amazon.

COMFORT

This is the #1 question people ask, and the answer depends on what you mean by "comfortable."

The 2002R uses ABZORB cushioning in the midsole, which gives it a soft, pillowy feel right out of the box. It's immediately comfortable with no break-in period. The N-ergy technology in the heel adds bounce. It feels like walking on a cloud — but it's not the most supportive shoe for long periods on your feet.

The 990v6 uses FuelCell foam, which is more responsive and structured. It doesn't feel as soft initially, but it provides better arch support and all-day comfort. The 990 was literally designed as a running shoe — it's engineered for performance. After a few wears, it molds to your foot perfectly.

Comfort Verdict

Immediate comfort: 2002R wins
All-day support: 990 wins
Standing/walking: 990 wins
Casual lounging: 2002R wins

Both come in standard and wide widths. The 990 has better width options since it's part of NB's core performance line. For more details, see our complete sizing guide.

STYLE

The 2002R has a chunkier, more fashion-forward silhouette. It's the one you see on Instagram and in streetwear fits. The Protection Pack colorways (Rain Cloud, Phantom, Mirage Grey) are some of the most popular sneakers in the resale market. It reads younger and trendier.

The 990 is the "dad shoe" that became a fashion icon. It's cleaner, more understated, and works in more settings — from casual Fridays at the office to weekend errands. Steve Jobs wore 993s. The 990 line has a timeless quality that the 2002R hasn't earned yet.

Style Verdict

Streetwear / trendy: 2002R wins
Versatile / timeless: 990 wins
Collaborations: Both strong (JFG, ALD, Kith)
Colorway variety: 2002R has more options

DURABILITY

The 990 lasts significantly longer. Made in USA construction, higher-quality suede, and a more durable outsole mean you can wear a pair of 990s for 2-3 years of regular use. The 2002R uses thinner materials and the ABZORB midsole compresses faster — expect 1-1.5 years of regular wear before the cushioning feels flat.

WHICH SHOULD YOU BUY?

Budget under $160: 2002R — best value in NB lineup
All-day comfort: 990v6 — superior support
Streetwear fits: 2002R — chunkier silhouette
Everyday beater: 990v6 — built to last
First New Balance: 2002R — lower risk at lower price
Investment piece: 990v6 — holds value better

Both are excellent shoes. The 2002R is the smarter buy if you're budget-conscious or want a trendy sneaker. The 990 is the better shoe overall if you're willing to spend $200. Compare all New Balance prices on SneakyRadar.
