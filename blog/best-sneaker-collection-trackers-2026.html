BEST SNEAKER COLLECTION TRACKERS

Updated Mar 2026 · SneakyRadar
Best Collection Trackers 2026

If you own more than a few pairs of sneakers, you've probably wondered what your collection is actually worth. Sneaker collection trackers solve that — they let you log your kicks, pull current market values, and see your total portfolio at a glance. Here's how the top options compare in 2026.

TL;DR: If you want free and simple, SneakyRadar's collection tracker works in your browser with no downloads or fees. If you want a full-featured mobile app with wear tracking and community features, Unboxed is the premium option at $9.99/month.

THE COMPARISON

SneakyRadar Collection Tracker

Price: Free
Platform: Browser (any device)
Download: None required

Features: Search and add sneakers from a database of thousands. Real-time market values. Profit/loss per pair. Total portfolio value. Sort by value, date, or profit. Share your collection with a public link. Syncs across devices. Integrated with SneakyRadar's price comparison tool.

Best for: Casual collectors who want a quick, free way to see what their collection is worth without downloading anything or paying monthly.

Unboxed

Price: $9.99/month
Platform: iOS, Android
Download: App required

Features: Collection tracking with barcode scanning. Wear tracking (log when you wear each pair). Community features — share wears, give and receive props. Authentication service. Drop and restock alerts. Reddit Sneakermarket integration for deals by size. Collection value tracking.

Best for: Dedicated sneakerheads who want a full ecosystem — wear tracking, community, authentication, and alerts in one app. Worth the subscription if you use all the features.

SoleDeck

Price: $7.99/month
Platform: iOS, Android, Web
Download: App available

Features: Collection management with ROI analysis. Real-time value tracking. Market trend insights. Public profile to showcase collection. Investment opportunity identification. Historical data analysis.

Best for: Collectors who treat sneakers as investments and want detailed ROI analytics and market trend data.

Sneakerr

Price: $4.99/month
Platform: iOS, Android
Download: App required

Features: Sneaker scanner — scan a shoe and get the exact model. Price comparison across shops. Favorites and wishlist management. Collection organization.

Best for: People who want quick sneaker identification and price checking on the go. The scanner feature is unique.

COLLECT by SoleSavy

Price: Free (with paid membership tiers)
Platform: iOS, Android
Download: App required

Features: Collection tracking with wear logging. Peer-to-peer marketplace with lowest fees in the industry. Free trades between members. Community-focused with buying/selling built in.

Best for: Collectors who want to buy, sell, and trade within a community. The marketplace integration is the differentiator.

WHICH ONE SHOULD YOU USE?

If you just want to know what your collection is worth without paying anything or downloading an app, SneakyRadar is the move. Open it in your browser, add your kicks, done.

If you're a serious collector who wants wear tracking, community features, and authentication, Unboxed justifies the $10/month if you use everything it offers.

If you're treating sneakers as investments, SoleDeck's ROI analytics and market trend tools are the most detailed.

If you want to buy, sell, and trade with other collectors, COLLECT by SoleSavy has the best marketplace integration.
