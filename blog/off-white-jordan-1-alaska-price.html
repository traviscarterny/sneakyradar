WHY THIS SHOE IS WORTH $500+

The original Off-White x Air Jordan 1 "The Ten" from 2017 is one of the most significant sneaker collaborations ever made. The Chicago colorway resells for $5,000+. The "Alaska" was the European-exclusive version that most people never had access to.

Virgil Abloh passed away in November 2021. The "V.A.A." branding on this release makes it part of the Virgil Abloh Archive collection, adding emotional and cultural significance beyond just a reissue. This isn't just a sneaker — it's a piece of fashion history.
