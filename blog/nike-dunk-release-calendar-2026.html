THE DUNK MARKET IN 2026

Dunk Low
SB Dunk
The split: GR Dunks sit below retail while SB Dunks command premium resale

There's a clear two-tier market for Dunks in 2026. General release Dunk Lows — the colorways you can find at Foot Locker — are sitting at or below retail on the secondary market. The Panda, for example, trades for roughly half its retail price on StockX.

On the other hand, Nike SB Dunks are thriving. Limited production runs, skate shop exclusives, and collaborations are consistently hitting 100-200% above retail. If you're interested in Dunks as an investment, SB is where the value is.

Nike CEO Elliott Hill's strategy of reducing output is starting to show results. As fewer Dunks hit the market through 2026, expect general release resale prices to stabilize and eventually climb back toward retail.
