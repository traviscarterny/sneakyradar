WHY THIS RELEASE MATTERS

The Air Jordan 13 is the shoe Michael Jordan wore during the 1997-98 "Last Dance" season. The Chicago colorway — white leather with red suede — is one of the OG colorways tied directly to MJ's championship legacy. This is only the third time it's been released, making it relatively scarce compared to other retros.

The 2017 version now trades at $250-$350 in used condition. The 2026 retro at $215 retail with resell projected at $280+ looks strong for both wearing and holding.
