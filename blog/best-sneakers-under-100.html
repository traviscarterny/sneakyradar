HOW TO FIND THE BEST DEALS

Prices on resale platforms change daily. Here's how to consistently find sneakers under $100:

1. Compare across platforms. A shoe listed at $95 on StockX might be $82 on GOAT or $78 on eBay. Use SneakyRadar to compare all platforms instantly.

2. Check smaller sizes and larger sizes. The most common sizes (9-11) are usually the most expensive. Size 7 or size 13+ often sit 20-30% cheaper.

3. Wait for restocks. When Nike or adidas restocks a shoe, resale prices drop for 1-2 weeks. That's your buying window.

4. Look at "used" on GOAT. GOAT's used section has sneakers in great condition for 30-50% less than deadstock prices.
