SIZING & FIT

The Vomero 5 runs true to size. The midfoot is slightly narrow due to the cage overlay, but the toe box has decent room. If you have wide feet, half size up is recommended. The shoe breaks in after a few wears — the initial tightness loosens up. Check our complete sizing guide for more details.

WHY THE VOMERO 5 IS WORTH BUYING

Unlike most fashion sneakers, the Vomero 5 is genuinely comfortable. It was designed as a premium running shoe with Zoom Air cushioning — you're getting real technology, not just a shape. The futuristic design stands out without being loud, and the price point is reasonable compared to New Balance 990s or designer sneakers. At $140-$180 for a general release colorway, it's one of the best values in the current sneaker market.
