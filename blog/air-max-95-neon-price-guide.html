2025 LIMITED VS 2026 BIG BUBBLE

Nike released the Air Max 95 Neon in limited quantities in 2025 for the 30th anniversary. That version quickly sold out and now trades at $300+. The 2026 Big Bubble version has a wider release with more stock, meaning lower resell prices but easier access for people who actually want to wear the shoe.

The key difference is the air window — the 2026 Big Bubble has a visibly larger heel air unit that's closer to Sergio Lozano's original prototype design. If you're a collector, both versions are worth having. If you just want to rock the Neon colorway, the 2026 version is the better buy.
