PRICE TRACKING ON INDIVIDUAL PLATFORMS

StockX Bids

How it works: Place a bid below market price. If a seller accepts, you buy.
Pro: Can save $20-50 vs buying at asking price
Con: No guarantee your bid gets filled. Fees apply.

GOAT "Want" Feature

How it works: Set your target price. GOAT notifies you when it's available.
Pro: Passive — set it and forget it
Con: Only tracks GOAT prices, not other platforms

eBay Saved Searches

How it works: Save a search. Get email alerts when new listings match.
Pro: eBay prices are often the lowest. Authenticity Guarantee included.
Con: Email alerts can be noisy. Need to filter manually.

THE SMART BUYING STRATEGY

Here's the approach that saves the most money:

1. Search on SneakyRadar to see all prices at once
2. Check eBay Authenticity Guarantee listings first — usually cheapest
3. Place a StockX bid 10-15% below the lowest ask
4. Set a GOAT "Want" at your target price as backup
5. Wait 2-4 weeks after release day — prices almost always dip
